Hello

Hello, my name is Saaya Fukuoka.

I am currently majoring in Applied Mathematics at UC San Diego, while also focusing on Data Science and Computer Science. Initially, I was interested in medicine, but an experience during my study abroad in France made me realize that data has the potential to protect lives and improve daily living. Since then, I have been drawn to the idea of contributing to society through the power of mathematics and data.

I have strengthened my analytical and problem-solving skills by applying my knowledge of machine learning and statistical modeling to real-world data.
Having lived in Japan, France, and the U.S., I have also developed strong cross-cultural communication skills and the ability to collaborate with people from diverse backgrounds.

Moving forward, I hope to continue deepening my learning by focusing not only on the numbers, but also on the human stories and behaviors behind the data.
